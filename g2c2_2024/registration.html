The registration includes one basic step and several optional steps marked with '+'. We kindly ask you to write a separate email for each step you need.

To register please send an email with subject 'Registration' and containing your first name, last name, country, city and affiliation to goryainov@hebtu.edu.cn by June 1st, 2024.

 +If you need a visa to China for G2C2 please send a separate email entitled 'Visa' to goryainov@hebtu.edu.cn containing your personal information (please see the form below) and a scanned copy of your id/passport in the attachment so that we could prepare an invitation letter for you. If you travel with an accompanying person who needs a visa to China, you are welcome to send the personal information of the accompanying person as well.

 +If you need assistance in booking your accommodation, please have a look at our suggestions in Accommodation, and write an email with subject 'Accommodation' to goryainov@hebtu.edu.cn by June 1st, 2024 and specify the dates of your staying. Note that the organisers will cover the accommodation of all students in twin rooms (shared with another participant). If you are a student, please explicitly mention this in your message and request the coverage of your accommodation.

 +If you want to contribute a talk, please prepare your one-page abstract with the template. Then send an email with subject 'Abstract' and provided with '.tex' file to goryainov@hebtu.edu.cn by June 1st, 2024.
